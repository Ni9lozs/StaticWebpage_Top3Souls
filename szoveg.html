What exactly happens in the video

Trailer of the game. In the beginning of the video, we are in some mystical corridor, full of prison cells. Then, we see the hero of the game, who is locked up in prison. He is an undead, which is a kind of curse, the person cannot die. Then, we see Firelink Shrine, which is basically the centre of the game. There, a knight tells you, you have to ring the two bells of awakening, and visualizes the things you have to go through. There are huge monsters that kill you with a thorn, and catacombs where you can be killed by skeletons, or even getting killed by magic. You can see fire monsters underneath the ground, magical knights, and dragons as well. Then, the knight tells you that you may link to the fire, which is basically you sacrifising yourself in order to break the curse.
