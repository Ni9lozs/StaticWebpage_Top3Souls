Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order

About the game

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is an action-adventure game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts. It was released for Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in November 2019, for Stadia in November 2020, and for the PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X and Series S in June 2021. The story, set in the Star Wars universe five years after Star Wars: Episode III â€“ Revenge of the Sith, follows Jedi Padawan Cal Kestis, who becomes a target of the Galactic Empire and is hunted throughout the galaxy by two Inquisitors while attempting to complete his training, escape his troubled past, and rebuild the fallen Jedi Order.

The game was announced during E3 2018, with a more detailed reveal during the Star Wars Celebration in April 2019. The game received generally favourable reviews from critics and was a commercial success, selling more than ten million copies by 2020. A sequel is in development.
